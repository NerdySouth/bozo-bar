# BozoBar Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** April 1, 2026

BozoBar does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data,
usage data, analytics, or telemetry of any kind.

## What BozoBar does

BozoBar communicates directly with your Bose headphones over Bluetooth
Low Energy. All communication happens locally between your Mac and your
headphones. No data leaves your device.

## Third-party services

BozoBar does not use any third-party services, SDKs, analytics frameworks,
or advertising networks.

## Contact

If you have questions about this policy, open an issue at
[github.com/NerdySouth/bozo-bar](https://github.com/NerdySouth/bozo-bar/issues).
